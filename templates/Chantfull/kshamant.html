Kshama Mantra


करचरण कृतं वाक्कायजं कर्मजं वा । श्रवणनयनजं वा मानसं वापराधं । विहितमविहितं वा सर्वमेतत्क्षमस्व । जय जय करुणाब्धे श्रीमहादेव शम्भो ॥

karacarana krtam vakkayajam karmajam va. sravananayanajam va manasam vaparadham. vihitamavihitam va sarvametatksamasva. jaya jaya karunabdhe srimahadeva sambho

O Compassionate Mahadeva! O Sambho! Victory to You! Please do forgive all the errors[अपराधम्] committed by me with the hands or legs[करचरण कृतं ], speech and body [वाक कायजम्], through actions[कर्म], through the ears[श्रवण], eyes[नयन], mind[मानस] or by not doing ordained[विहितम्] actions or by doing prohibited actions.
