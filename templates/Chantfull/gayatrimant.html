Gayatri Mantra


ॐ भूर्भुवः स्वः तत्सवितुर्वरेण्यं भर्गो देवस्य धीमहि धियो यो नः प्रचोदयात् ॥

Om Bhuur-Bhuvah Svah Tat-Savitur-Varennyam Bhargo Devasya Dhiimahi Dhiyo Yo Nah Pracodayaat ||

Om, Pervading the Bhu Loka , Bhuvar Loka and Swar Loka, 2: That Savitur [Divine Essence of the Sun] which is the most Adorable, 3: I Meditate on that Divine Effulgence, 4: May that Awaken our Intelligence.
