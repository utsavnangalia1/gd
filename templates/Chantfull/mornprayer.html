Beautiful Prayer for Morning/ [Prithvi Sagandha Sarasa]


पृथ्वी सगंधा सरसाः तथाऽपः स्पर्शी च वायु ज्वलितं च तेजः। नभः सशब्दं महता सहैव कुर्वतु सर्वे मम सुप्रभातम् ॥

prithvi sagamdhaa sarasah tatha-paha sparsi ca vaayu jvalitam ca tejaha. nabhaha sashabdam mahataa sahaiva kurvatu sarve mama suprabhaatam

The fragrant earth, the waters of reservoirs, the touch of the wind, the brilliance of the fire and the great voice of the akasa, may all of them make mine a good morning.
