About GyaanDweep

GyaanDweep is a one-stop Platform for the world where we explore interactive & extensive discoveries with knowledge on Scientific as well as Spiritual disciplines which existed in the Vedic Era. This initiative is a step forward in bringing the divine knowledge & practices to light and making bharat a vishwaguru. We are striving to raise awareness among people in our quest to make Bharat Aatmanirbhar and lead the way forward for a better world.
