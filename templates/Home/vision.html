Our Vision-Inspired by Kundalini

Please read from below so you can realise the message.

Learning from the root to tip of the Kundalini and after realising the self, we are determined to create a bridge between People of Knowledge[Brahmins,Sage & Seers] and Student of Today's World. With this bridge, we hope that students on the vedic path seek information,Knowledge and Wisdom in their learning with the help of services and features provided by the company and receive continous guidance in their path of Yoga.
After realising the purpose of Vishudhi Chakra, we raise our consciousness and reach the Ajna Chakra or Third Eye Chakra. Ajna literally means a command and represents the Manas[Mind] element. Inspired by this, we perceive that the 21st Century is the Indian Century and to lead the way forward, we need to prepare our youth to be skillful in their respective interests.
After realising the purpose of Anahata Chakra, we raise our consciousness and reach the Vishuddhi Chakra or Throat Chakra. Vishudhi literally means Purifier and represents the Akasha Element. Inspired by this, we are adament in bringing out unbiased purified content without any malice or profane language and make our platform the best of Spaces.
After realising the purpose of Manipura Chakra, we raise our consciousness and reach Anahata Chakra or Heart Chakra. Anahata means "Unstruck Sound" , signifies Love and Compassion and represents Air Element. Inspired by this, we will persevere in raising flowing awareness for Vedic Literature with love for the Sanskrit Language and lead the way forward for Bharat being a "VishwaGuru" with compassion.
After realising the purpose of SvAdthisThAna Chakra, we raise our consciousness and reach Manipura or Navel Chakra. Manipura Chakra signifies Ambition, Hunger, Nourishment and represents Fire Element. Inspired by this, we are ambitious in being the best platform for Human Self Development. We are equally devoted in keeping our hunger in check by monitoring the fire in our bellies.
After Realising the purpose of Muladhara Chakra, we rise above to SvAdthisThAna Chakra or Sacral Chkara. SvAdthisThAna literally means "Well Established" and represents Water Element which signifies "Flow". Inspired by this, we are persistent in bringing constant Flow of Content for the users and later becoming "Well Established" in this vertical.
Muladhara Chakra or Root Chakra is the feet of the Kundalini where "Mul" means Source and "Adhara" means support. So mooladhara literally means "Source Support" and it represents the Earth Element which signifies "Grounding". Inspired by this, we are poised to stay grounded in our purpose to become the "Source" of great Vedic Content and Support the learning of Fellow Learners.
