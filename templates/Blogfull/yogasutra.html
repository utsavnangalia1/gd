#Yogasutra/[योग् सूत्रः]

Patanjali Yoga Sutra-Path between human and divine consciousness.

Patanjali Yoga Sutras is one of the most beautiful out of many work by Sage Patanjali. Yoga Sutras are one of the most translated texts in history of Sanatana Dharma apart from Shrimad Bhagvad Gita.It was translated in more than 40 languages.The texts contain almost 196 sutras or aphorisms which details out everything about Yoga.

Sage Patanjali wrote this text around 500BC and compiled his learning and observations from yoga. Sutras in sanskrit means a thread, a thread which holds the meaning of the discipline in study,together. The meaning can be different according to a yogi, but the crux or the thread which hold this meaning together remains undisturbed.

Sutras are small texts, around 10-20 devanagari letters each. But the meaning which the sutra represent is magnanimous. Each sutra when broken down and understood can be stretched into multiple page depending on the understanding a yogi holds.

Let us understand the significance,meaning and symbolism behind patanjali Yoga Sutras.
